# Structural Metabolism — Tier 3 Draft

## Meta-Structural Description

The minimal system that can both maintain and transform structure. ISC provides the homeostatic phase — convergence to a declared target state despite perturbation. RST provides the phase-transition operation — controlled dissolution of current form to enable recomposition at a different scale. Together they form a metabolic cycle: ISC maintains structural integrity within a phase, RST executes the transition between phases, and the alternation between them constitutes the system's structural metabolism.

## Geometric Relationship

ISC and RST are **dual operations** on structure:
- ISC: perturbation → convergence → same state (structure-preserving)
- RST: fitness threshold crossed → dissolution → recomposition (structure-transforming)

The duality is not merely conceptual — it is operationally complementary. A system that has ISC without RST becomes rigid (cannot adapt when its form becomes unfit). A system that has RST without ISC cannot maintain coherence between transitions. The coupling of both creates a system capable of sustained structural evolution: stable phases punctuated by controlled transitions.

## Cross-Domain Evidence

The ISC+RST coupling appears in:
- **Cell biology**: homeostasis (ISC) + apoptosis (RST) — the organism maintains cellular stability while selectively dissolving unfit cells
- **Software architecture**: idempotent deployment (ISC) + circuit breaker patterns (RST) — the system converges to declared state while shedding failed components
- **Ecology**: climax community stability (ISC) + controlled burns / succession reset (RST) — the ecosystem maintains structure while periodically dissolving accumulated fuel loads
- **Law**: legal certainty / stare decisis (ISC) + bankruptcy restructuring (RST) — the legal system maintains precedent stability while enabling controlled dissolution of insolvent entities

## Falsification Conditions

- If ISC and RST are shown to operate independently in a majority of systems (i.e., systems typically have one without the other), the coupling claim fails and this is two separate motifs, not a meta-motif.
- If the "metabolic cycle" framing adds no predictive power beyond what ISC and RST individually provide, this is a post-hoc label rather than a genuine Tier 3 pattern.
- If a system can achieve sustained structural evolution through a mechanism that is neither ISC-like nor RST-like, the claim that these two are the "minimal" metabolism is falsified.
