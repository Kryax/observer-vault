**Key findings:**
- Operator space is a three-ray structure (D/I/R) with continuous depth (derivative order). Operators cluster between orders 1 and 2.
- D-axis has internal degeneracy at order 2 (CPA and ESMB co-located). I-axis has clean stratification (ISC order 1, DSG order 2). R-axis has interval-valued depth (OFL at 1-2).
