# Primitive Self-Reference

## Structural Description

The simplest form of recursion: a system that refers to itself without any derivative complexity. Not self-modification, not self-improvement, not developmental depth — just raw self-reference as a primitive. The system points at itself. This is the ground state of recursion, analogous to how Structural Coupling is the ground state of integration.

## Domain-Independent Formulation

Recursion at its zeroth order is bare self-reference: a system whose output includes a representation of itself, prior to any self-modification or developmental depth.
