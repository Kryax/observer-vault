# Disharmony Detection

## Governance Role
procedure

## Detection Signals

Structural disharmony is detected via:

- unresolved constraint conflicts
- missing dependencies
- forced or strained logic
- repeated clarification loops
- detail repeatedly breaking assumptions
- excessive explanation required to hold structure together

## Output

Detection produces:

STATE: DISHARMONY_DETECTED

Evidence and impact must be listed.

Detection does not grant action authority.
